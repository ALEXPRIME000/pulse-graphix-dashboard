‚ö° Contrast Specifications

YouTube thumbnails compete with hundreds of other videos. HIGH CONTRAST is essential for visibility.

‚úÖ GOOD CONTRAST

BLACK TEXT ON BRIGHT YELLOW

WCAG AAA Rating: 19.56:1

‚ùå POOR CONTRAST

GREY ON GREY

WCAG Rating: 2.1:1 (Fails)

Contrast Best Practices:

  • Minimum Ratio: 7:1 for text (WCAG AAA standard)
  • Text Stroke: Add 8-12px white or black stroke around text for readability on any background
  • Background Split: Use contrasting halves (e.g., bright left, dark right) to separate elements
  • Avoid: Light text on light backgrounds, dark on dark
  • Test: Convert thumbnail to grayscale ‚Äî if elements disappear, contrast is too low

High-Impact Color Combinations:

  • Black text + Yellow/Orange background
  • White text + Dark blue/purple background
  • White text with black stroke + Any vibrant background
  • Complementary colors: Blue/Orange, Purple/Yellow, Red/Green

‚úçÔ∏è Typography Recommendations

Primary Text (3-5 Words Max)

  • Font: Bebas Neue, Montserrat Black, Impact, or Oswald Bold
  • Size: 120-180px (HUGE for mobile visibility)
  • Weight: Extra Bold / Black
  • Stroke: 10-15px white or black outline
  • Shadow: 5px drop shadow for depth (offset: 5px, blur: 10px)
  • Color: High contrast with background
  • Max Characters: 20-30 characters total (shorter is better)

Secondary Text (Optional)

  • Font: Same as primary but lighter weight
  • Size: 60-80px
  • Use For: Supporting detail, context

Branding / Logo

  • Size: 80-120px height
  • Placement: Bottom-left or top-left corner
  • Margin: 40px from edges
