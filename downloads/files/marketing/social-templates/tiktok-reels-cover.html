Critical Guidelines:

  • Feed Preview Zone: Center your key visual and text in the MIDDLE 600px height (approx. pixels 660-1260 from top)
  • Top Area: Top ~180px can be obscured by profile picture, username, and "Follow" button
  • Bottom Area: Bottom ~480px contains caption, engagement buttons (like, comment, share), and audio info
  • Right Edge: Right ~60px reserved for vertical button stack ‚Äî avoid placing text here
  • The Golden Rule: Your cover must be compelling when viewed in grid/feed format (middle section only)
üì± Mobile-First Design: 99% of TikTok/Reels views happen on mobile. Always design and test at mobile scale. What looks good on desktop might be invisible on a phone.

‚úçÔ∏è Typography Recommendations

Primary Headline (2-4 Words)

  • Font: Montserrat Black, Bebas Neue, or Anton
  • Size: 100-140px (LARGE for mobile visibility)
  • Weight: Extra Bold / Black
  • Stroke: 8-12px contrasting outline (white on dark, black on light)
  • Placement: Centered vertically in feed safe zone (middle 600px)
  • Line Height: 1.1 (tight spacing for impact)
  • Max Lines: 2-3 lines maximum

Secondary Text (Optional)

  • Font: Open Sans Bold or Roboto Bold
  • Size: 48-64px
  • Use For: Supporting context, episode number, category
  • Placement: Above or below headline, within safe zone

Branding / Logo

  • Size: 60-90px height
  • Placement: Top-left or top-center (if above safe zone) OR bottom-left (if below safe zone)
  • Margin: 40px from edges
  • Opacity: Consider 70-80% opacity if overlaying video content
‚ö†Ô∏è Text Readability: Vertical videos scroll fast. If someone can't read your text in 0.5 seconds, it's too complex. Keep it BOLD, SHORT, and HIGH CONTRAST.
