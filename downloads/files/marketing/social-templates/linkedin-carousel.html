‚úçÔ∏è Typography Recommendations

Primary Headlines (Slide Titles)

  • Font: Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold, or Inter Bold
  • Size: 64-80px
  • Line Height: 1.2
  • Color: High contrast with background (black on light, white on dark)
  • Max Characters: 40-50 characters per line (2-3 lines max)

Body Text

  • Font: Open Sans Regular, Roboto Regular, or Inter Regular
  • Size: 32-40px
  • Line Height: 1.5
  • Max Lines: 6-8 lines per slide

Supporting Text / Captions

  • Font: Same as body, but lighter weight or smaller size
  • Size: 24-28px
  • Use For: Sources, footnotes, slide numbers

üìã Carousel Structure Best Practices

  1. Slide 1 (Hook): Eye-catching title, question, or bold statement
  2. Slides 2-6 (Content): One key point per slide, use numbers or icons
  3. Slide 7 (Summary): Recap key takeaways
  4. Slide 8 (CTA): Call-to-action (Follow, Comment, Link in bio, DM)
