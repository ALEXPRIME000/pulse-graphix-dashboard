‚úçÔ∏è Typography Recommendations

Quote Text (Main Content)

  • Font: Merriweather (serif), Playfair Display (elegant), or Montserrat (modern sans-serif)
  • Size: 52-72px (depends on quote length)
  • Weight: Bold or Semi-Bold
  • Line Height: 1.4-1.6
  • Alignment: Center (most common) or Left-aligned with visual balance
  • Color: White or very light color (if dark background), Dark color (if light background)
  • Max Characters: 80-120 characters (15-20 words optimal)

Quote Marks (Decorative)

  • Font: Serif font (Georgia, Playfair Display) for classic look
  • Size: 120-180px
  • Opacity: 20-40% (subtle, not overpowering)
  • Placement: Top-left and/or bottom-right corners

Attribution / Author Name

  • Font: Same as quote or complementary sans-serif
  • Size: 28-36px
  • Weight: Regular or Medium
  • Style: Preceded by em dash "‚Äî" or "‚Äì"
  • Placement: Bottom-center or bottom-right
  • Opacity: 85-100%

Brand Name / Logo Text

  • Size: 20-28px (if text) or 60-80px (if logo)
  • Placement: Opposite corner from attribution (e.g., top-right if attribution is bottom-left)
  • Opacity: 70-90% (subtle branding)

‚ú® Design Best Practices

  • ‚úÖ Keep It Short: 15-20 words maximum ‚Äî quote cards are meant to be read quickly
  • ‚úÖ High Contrast: Quote must be instantly readable ‚Äî no squinting
  • ‚úÖ Breathing Room: Don't cram text ‚Äî white space (or background space) is your friend
  • ‚úÖ Consistent Branding: Use brand colors and fonts for recognition
  • ‚úÖ Subtle Logo: Logo should be present but not competing with the quote
  • ‚úÖ Shareable: Make it so good people want to repost it
  • ‚ùå Avoid Clutter: Don't add unnecessary decorative elements
  • ‚ùå Avoid Bad Typography: No comic sans, no overly decorative fonts that hurt readability
  • ‚ùå Avoid Low Contrast: Light grey text on white = invisible

üí° Quote Selection Tips

Great Quotes Are:

  • Short: Under 20 words (fits comfortably on 1080√ó1080px)
  • Impactful: Makes you think or feel something
  • Relevant: Aligned with your brand values and audience interests
  • Shareable: People want to repost it to their stories/feeds
  • Authentic: Either original or properly attributed

Quote Types That Perform Well:

  • Inspirational/motivational quotes
  • Industry wisdom (design, AI, business)
  • Client testimonials (with permission)
  • Company mission/vision statements
  • Thought-provoking questions
  • Before/after mindset shifts
