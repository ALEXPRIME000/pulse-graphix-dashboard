‚úçÔ∏è Typography Recommendations

Category Label (Top Header)

  • Font: Montserrat Bold, Bebas Neue, or Oswald Bold
  • Size: 36-48px
  • Weight: Bold or Extra Bold
  • Letter Spacing: +2-4px (tracking for emphasis)
  • Color: White (if on colored header) or brand color
  • Examples: "NEW LAUNCH", "LIMITED TIME", "COMING SOON", "BIG NEWS"

Main Headline

  • Font: Montserrat Bold, Poppins Bold, or custom brand font
  • Size: 64-80px
  • Weight: Bold
  • Line Height: 1.2
  • Color: High contrast (dark on light, light on dark)
  • Max Length: 3-6 words (1-2 lines)

Supporting Text / Details

  • Font: Open Sans Regular, Roboto Regular
  • Size: 32-40px
  • Line Height: 1.5
  • Color: Medium contrast (#666 on white, rgba(255,255,255,0.8) on dark)
  • Max Length: 20-30 words (2-3 lines)

CTA Button Text

  • Font: Montserrat Bold or Poppins Semi-Bold
  • Size: 40-52px
  • Weight: Bold or Semi-Bold
  • Letter Spacing: +1-2px
  • Color: White (on colored button) or very high contrast
  • Max Length: 2-4 words (e.g., "LEARN MORE", "SHOP NOW", "REGISTER FREE")

Date/Time/Location Text

  • Font: Roboto Medium or Open Sans Semi-Bold
  • Size: 28-32px
  • Style: Often with icon (üìÖ calendar, üïê clock, üìç pin)

üìã Announcement Types & Variations

  • Header: "BIG NEWS" or "ANNOUNCEMENT"
  • Content: Update summary, implications, next steps
  • CTA: "READ MORE", "LEARN ABOUT IT", "SEE WHAT'S NEW"

5. Coming Soon Teaser

  • Header: "COMING SOON"
  • Content: Teaser text, launch date, sneak peek visual
  • CTA: "JOIN WAITLIST", "BE THE FIRST", "GET NOTIFIED"
