# Social Media Templates — Design Specifications
*Prepared: Feb 26, 2026 | For Kabundji to build in Canva/Figma*

---

## Master Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary Dark | #0D0D0D | Backgrounds |
| Primary Teal | #14B8A6 | Accent, CTAs, icons |
| Secondary Sky | #0EA5E9 | Gradients, highlights |
| Gold | #F59E0B | Revenue/growth elements |
| White | #FFFFFF | Text on dark |
| Muted | #888888 | Secondary text |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter 700-900
- **Body:** Inter 400-500
- **Small/Captions:** Inter 300, uppercase, tracking wider

## Template 1: LinkedIn Carousel
**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080px (square) or 1080 x 1350px (portrait)
**Format:** 5-7 slides

**Slide Structure:**
1. **Hook Slide:** Big number + problem statement
   - "73% des sites PME ne convertissent pas"
   - Dark bg (#0D0D0D), teal accent bar left

2-4. **Content Slides:** Tips numbered 1, 2, 3
   - Bullet points with teal icons
   - White text, generous whitespace

5. **CTA Slide:** "Voir mon portfolio →" with website
   - Gradient bg (teal to sky), white text

**Design Notes:**
- Consistent header bar with "Pulse Graphix" logo
- Page numbers bottom right (01/05...)
- Arrow icon "→" after each CTA

---

## Template 2: YouTube Thumbnail
**Dimensions:** 1280 x 720px
**Safe Zone:** 1280 x 420px (title must fit here)

**Layout:**
- **Left 60%:** Your photo (professional, looking at camera, confident)
- **Right 40%:** Big title text + small "Pulse Graphix" watermark
- **Background:** Dark gradient (#0D0D0D → #1a1a1a) with subtle pattern
- **Accent:** Teal underline or corner element

**Title Treatment:**
- All caps, bold, white with subtle shadow
- Max 6 words
- Example: "CONSTRUIRE UN SITE EN 30 MIN"

**Watermark:** Bottom right, small Pulse Graphix logo, semi-transparent

---

## Template 3: TikTok Cover / Instagram Reels
**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920px (9:16 vertical)

**Frame (15 sec video setup):**
- **0-3 sec:** Hook text + face
- **3-12 sec:** Screen recording/demo
- **12-15 sec:** CTA with handle

**Cover Frame:**
- Large emoji/illustration top center
- Big text: "3 ASTUCES SITES WEB"
- "Pulse Graphix" bottom, small
- Teal highlight marks on key words

---

## Template 4: Quote Card
**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080px (square)

**Layout:**
- **Split:** Left 40% teal gradient box, Right 60% dark
- **Left:** Small "Pulse Graphix" vertical
- **Right:** Quote in white italic text
- **Bottom:** Author name + small headshot circle

**Quote Styling:**
- Large quotation mark in teal (top left of text area)
- Text: "Votre site web est votre meilleur vendeur. S'il dort, vous perdez des clients."
- Font size: 36-48px

---

## Template 5: Announcement / Launch
**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1350px (portrait for LinkedIn/IG)

**Structure:**
1. **Header:** "NOUVEAU" badge + date
2. **Main Visual:** Product screenshot or service illustration
3. **Headline:** 2-3 words, huge, bold
4. **Body:** 2-3 lines of details
5. **Footer:** CTA button + website

**Example — Template Launch:**
- Badge: "DISPONIBLE MAINTENANT" (teal bg)
- Visual: SaaS dashboard mockup
- Headline: "TEMPLATE SAAS" (gradient text)
- Body: "Prêt en 48h. 100% personnalisable."
- CTA: "VOIR LA DÉMO →"
