## Template 1: LinkedIn Carousel (5 Slides)

### Slide 1: Cover
- **Size:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (vertical)
- **Layout:** Bold single statement on dark gradient
- **Text:** "5 Things Your Website Is Missing"
- **Visual:** Subtle abstract wave or tech pattern in brand colors
- **CTA:** "Swipe to see →"

### Slide 2: Problem
- **Layout:** Split 60/40
- **Left:** Large number "1." in display font
- **Right:** "No clear call-to-action"
- **Visual:** Before/after icon treatment

### Slides 3-5: Remaining Problems
- **2.** "Slow mobile load times"
- **3.** "No booking/contact form"
- **4.** "Missing contact info in header"
- **5.** "No social proof/testimonials"

### Last Slide: CTA
- **Text:** "Need help fixing these?"
- **Button style:** "DM us 'WEBSITE' for a free audit"
- **Contact:** @chiccomutombo | pulsegraphix.studio

## Template 2: Quote Card / Motivational Post

### Layout A: Full Bleed
- **Size:** 1080x1080px
- **Background:** Duotone image (dark overlay)
- **Quote:** "A website should work while you sleep."
- **Attribution:** — @chiccomutombo (small, bottom right)
- **Logo:** Pulse Graphix watermarked

### Layout B: Clean Minimal
- **Size:** 1080x1080px
- **Background:** Solid color (deep navy or warm orange)
- **Quote typography:** Large serif font, centered
- **Decorative:** Thin line rule above/below quote

**Quote Bank:**
- "Good design is good business."
- "Your website is your 24/7 salesperson."
- "If your site loads slowly, you load slowly."
- "Mobile-first isn't a trend—it's reality."

## Template 3: YouTube Thumbnail

### Layout
- **Size:** 1280x720px
- **Style:** Bold, high contrast, readable at small sizes

### Elements
- **Left 60%:** Feature image or face (high emotion/expression)
- **Right 40%:** Large bold text (3-5 words max)
- **Bottom:** Logo + Episode number badge
- **Optional:** Arrow or circle highlighting key element

### Color Treatment
- **Option A:** Teal/cyan text on dark background
- **Option B:** Orange accent on white/light bg
- **Option C:** Black text on bright color block

### Text Examples
- "Build Website in 30 Min"
- "Secret No One Tells"
- "Stop Losing Clients"
- "Free Tools I Use"

## Template 4: TikTok/Reels Cover

### Layout
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **Safe zones:** Keep critical text in center 1080x1080 (avoid top/bottom 15%)

### Elements
- **Top:** Episode/series title bar
- **Center:** Large bold hook text (question or statement)
- **Bottom:** Subtitle + @handle
- **optional:** Arrow pointing to "Watch Now"

### Hook Examples
- "This one mistake costs you €5000/month"
- "POV: Your competitor just got online"
- "Before vs After Website Redesign"
- "3 things web designers won't tell you"

## Template 5: Announcement / Service Launch

### Layout
- **Size:** 1080x1080px or 1080x1350px
- **Style:** Premium, editorial feel

### Sections (top to bottom)
1. **Badge:** "NEW SERVICE" or "NOW AVAILABLE"
2. **Headline:** Service name (large, bold)
3. **Visual:** Hero image or abstract graphic
4. **Description:** 2-3 lines of benefit-focused copy
5. **CTA button:** "Book a call" or "Learn more"
6. **Stack:** "Web Design | Branding | Marketing"

### Examples
- **Service:** "Website in a Week"
  - "From concept to live site in 7 days. For businesses that can't wait."
- **Service:** "Design Audit"
  - "Is your website costing you customers? Get expert eyes on your site for €199."

## Design Specifications

### Color Palette (Pulse Graphix)
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | #FF6B35 | CTAs, highlights, accent |
| Secondary | #004E89 | Backgrounds, trust elements |
| Dark | #1A1A2E | Text, deep backgrounds |
| Light | #F7F9FC | Light backgrounds |
| Accent | #00D9FF | Links, badges, energy points |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Poppins Bold or Montserrat Bold
- **Body:** Open Sans or Inter Regular
- **Quotes:** Playfair Display or similar serif
