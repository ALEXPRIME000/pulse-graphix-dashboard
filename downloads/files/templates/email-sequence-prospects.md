## Usage Notes

### When to Send:
- **Email 1:** Immediately after first contact/call
- **Email 2:** 48-72 hours after Email 1 (if no reply)
- **Email 3:** 6-7 days after Email 2 (if no reply)
- **Email 4:** 30+ days later for dormant leads

### Customization Variables:
Replace these with actual data:
- `{{company_name}}` ‚Äì Prospect's business name
- `{{first_name}}` ‚Äì Contact person's first name
- `{{prospect_need_1/2/3}}` ‚Äì Specific needs they mentioned
- `{{industry}}` ‚Äì Their industry (restaurant, beauty, etc.)
- `{{specific_solution}}` ‚Äì What Pulse Graphix would build for them
- `{{estimated_leads}}` ‚Äì Estimated leads lost per week
- `{{estimated_revenue_loss}}` ‚Äì Dollar amount
- `{{case_study_company}}` ‚Äì Similar client we helped
- `{{percentage_increase}}` ‚Äì Percentage improvement we delivered
- `{{payback_period}}` ‚Äì How fast ROI was achieved
- `{{portfolio_link}}` ‚Äì Link to relevant portfolio item
- `{{delay_months}}` ‚Äì How many months to wait (3, 6, etc.)

### Tone Guidelines:
- Professional but warm ‚Äî we're creatives, not robots
- Value-focused, not pushy
- Respect their time (short emails)
- Always offer an easy out (never guilt-trip)
