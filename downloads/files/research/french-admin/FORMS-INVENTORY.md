## üßæ INCOME TAX FORMS (Impots.gouv.fr)

### Form 2042: Main Income Tax Return
- **Cerfa Number:** 10330*29 (year varies)
- **URL:** https://www.impots.gouv.fr/formulaire/2042/declaration-des-revenus
- **Platform:** impots.gouv.fr (online filing mandatory if internet-equipped)
- **Purpose:** Annual income declaration for all taxpayers
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Civil status: Name, first name, date/place of birth
  - Marital status and changes
  - Address and contact information
  - Tax household composition (dependents)
  - Employment income (wages, pensions)
  - Social security number (NIR)
  - Bank account details (RIB) for direct debit/refund
- **Annexes:**
  - 2042-C (complementary income: investments, capital gains)
  - 2042-C-PRO (professional income, BIC, BNC)
  - 2042-RICI (tax credits, deductions)
  - 2042-IOM (overseas income)

### Form 2044: Rental Income Declaration
- **Cerfa Number:** 10334*26
- **URL:** https://www.impots.gouv.fr/formulaire/2044/revenus-fonciers
- **Purpose:** Declaration of rental property income
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Property address and cadastral reference
  - Rental income received
  - Deductible expenses (repairs, management fees, property tax)
  - Loan interest payments
  - Co-owner percentage (if applicable)
  - Tenant information

### Form 2047: Foreign Income Declaration
- **Cerfa Number:** 11226*23
- **URL:** https://www.impots.gouv.fr/formulaire/2047/declaration-des-revenus-encaisses-letranger
- **Purpose:** Declaration of income earned abroad
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Country of income source
  - Nature of income (wages, pensions, rental, etc.)
  - Amount in foreign currency and euros
  - Taxes paid abroad
  - Tax treaty provisions applicable

### Form 6660: Property Tax Return
- **Cerfa Number:** 10517*21
- **URL:** https://www.impots.gouv.fr/formulaire/6660-rev/declaration-pour-lannee
- **Purpose:** Annual property tax declaration for changes
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Property identification (cadastral reference)
  - Nature of changes (construction, demolition, change of use)
  - Effective date of change
  - Property characteristics (surface area, rooms)

## üíº URSSAF FORMS (Social Security Contributions)

### Cerfa P0 PL: Liberal Profession Business Registration
- **Cerfa Number:** 11768*06
- **URL:** https://www.autoentrepreneur.urssaf.fr/ or https://formalites.entreprises.gouv.fr/
- **Purpose:** Register as liberal professional/auto-entrepreneur
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Personal information (name, birth date/place, address)
  - Social security number
  - Business activity description (APE code)
  - Business address
  - Start date of activity
  - Choice of tax regime
  - VAT regime option
  - ACRE (social security exemption) request
  - Bank details (RIB)
  - Professional qualification certificates

### Cerfa P2-P4: Business Modification/Cessation
- **Cerfa Number:** 11678*03 (modification), 11679*03 (cessation)
- **URL:** https://formalites.entreprises.gouv.fr/
- **Purpose:** Declare changes or closure of business
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - SIRET number
  - Nature of change (address, activity, name)
  - Effective date
  - Reason for cessation (if applicable)
  - Final revenue declaration

### D√©claration de Chiffre d'Affaires (Auto-Entrepreneur)

## üÜî IDENTITY & LIFE EVENT FORMS (Service-Public.fr)

### Cerfa 12100: National ID Card Application
- **Cerfa Number:** 12100*02
- **URL:** https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/R1073
- **Purpose:** Apply for or renew national identity card
- **Pre-application:** Online pre-application mandatory (ANTS)
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Full civil status
  - Address
  - Parent information (for minors)
  - Previous ID details (if renewal)
  - Reason for request (first time, renewal, loss, theft)
  - Emergency contact
- **Supporting documents needed:**
  - Birth certificate (< 3 months)
  - Proof of residence
  - 2 photos
  - Previous ID

### Cerfa 12100: Passport Application
- **Cerfa Number:** 12100*02 (same form as ID card)
- **URL:** https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/R42218
- **Pre-application:** Online (ANTS)
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Same as ID card application
  - Travel document history
  - Height, eye color
- **Note:** Tax stamp (timbre fiscal) required - purchased online

### Cerfa 14948: Driver's License Exchange
- **Cerfa Number:** 14948*01
- **URL:** https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/R23165
- **Platform:** ANTS (online only)
- **Purpose:** Exchange foreign license for French
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Original license details (number, country, issue date)
  - License categories held
  - Address in France
  - Duration of residence in France
  - Translation (if non-EU license)

### Cerfa 15725: Birth Declaration
- **Cerfa Number:** 15725*02
- **URL:** In-person at town hall (mairie)
- **Deadline:** 5 days from birth
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Child's name (first and last names)
  - Date, time, and place of birth
  - Gender
  - Parent information (names, occupations, addresses)
  - Marital status of parents
  - Witness information (if applicable)

### Cerfa 15725: PACS Declaration
- **Cerfa Number:** 15726*02
- **URL:** https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/R46735
- **Purpose:** Register a civil partnership
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Partners' full civil status
  - Address(es)
  - Previous marital status
  - Property regime choice
  - Convention of PACS (attached document)
  - Signatures of both partners

### Cerfa 11530: Marriage Publication
- **Cerfa Number:** 11530*03
- **Purpose:** Request publication of marriage banns
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Future spouses' information
  - Chosen date and location
  - Witness information (2-4 witnesses)
  - Previous marital status
  - Prenuptial agreement details (if applicable)

### Cerfa 13750: Change of Address Notification
- **Cerfa Number:** 13750*05
- **URL:** https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/R15248
- **Purpose:** Single form to notify multiple administrations
- **Platform:** service-public.fr
- **Required Data Fields:**
  - Old address
  - New address
  - Effective date of move
  - Contact information
  - Services to notify (checkboxes)
  - Tax number
  - Social security number
