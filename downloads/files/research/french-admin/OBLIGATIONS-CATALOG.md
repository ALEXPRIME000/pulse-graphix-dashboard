## üè• SOCIAL SECURITY OBLIGATIONS

### Health Insurance - CPAM (Assurance Maladie)
- **Registration:** Mandatory within 3 months of arrival/birth
- **Annual:** No annual filing, but update changes within 1 month
- **Key Events Requiring Notification:**
  - Change of address: Within 1 month
  - Change of employment: Within 1 month
  - Addition of beneficiaries: Within 1 month
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - Loss of healthcare coverage
  - Reimbursement delays
  - Potential retroactive payment of medical costs
- **Required documents:**
  - Birth certificate
  - Proof of residence
  - Social security number
  - Employment contract or proof of status

### Family Allowance Fund - CAF (Caisse d'Allocations Familiales)
- **Initial Application:** Within 3 months of qualifying event
- **Quarterly Income Declaration:** Every 3 months for RSA/prime d'activit√© recipients
- **Annual Resource Declaration:** January-February for previous year
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - Benefit suspension or reduction
  - Overpayment recovery (with interest)
  - Administrative fines
- **Required documents:**
  - Tax notice (avis d'imposition)
  - Birth certificates of children
  - Proof of residence (lease, utility bills)
  - Bank details (RIB)
  - Income statements (payslips)

### URSSAF (Social Contributions for Self-Employed)
- **Frequency:** Monthly or quarterly
- **Declaration:** Online via urssaf.fr or autoentrepreneur.urssaf.fr
- **Deadlines:** Varies by regime
  - Auto-entrepreneurs: Monthly (end of month) or quarterly
  - Liberal professions: Monthly/quarterly installments
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - 5% late payment penalty
  - 0.2% interest per month
  - Potential loss of social protection
- **Required documents:**
  - Revenue/turnover statements
  - SIRET number
  - Bank details

### Birth Declaration
- **Deadline:** Within 5 days of birth (3 days if born at home)
- **Where:** Town hall (mairie) of birth location
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - Criminal penalty (rare, but legally possible)
  - Complications for obtaining official documents
- **Required documents:**
  - Birth certificate from hospital
  - Parents' ID cards
  - Family record book (livret de famille) if exists
  - Marriage certificate (if applicable)

### Marriage (Mariage)
- **Notification:** Publication of banns 10 days before
- **Registration:** Immediate (day of ceremony)
- **Subsequent Updates Required:**
  - Tax authorities: Next tax filing
  - Social security: Within 1 month
  - CAF: Within 1 month
- **Required documents:**
  - Birth certificates (less than 3 months old)
  - Proof of residence
  - List of witnesses
  - Prenuptial agreement (if applicable)

### PACS (Civil Partnership)
- **Registration:** At town hall or notary
- **Updates Required:** Same as marriage (taxes, social security, CAF)
- **Dissolution:** Notify registering authority
- **Required documents:**
  - Birth certificates
  - Proof of residence
  - Joint declaration of PACS
  - ID documents

### Change of Address
- **Deadline:** No legal deadline, but recommended within 1 month
- **Entities to Notify:**
  - Tax authorities (impots.gouv.fr)
  - Social security (ameli.fr)
  - CAF (caf.fr)
  - Driver's license (ANTS)
  - Employer
  - Banks, insurance, utilities
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - Missed official correspondence
  - Service interruptions
  - Invalid license address (‚Ç¨135 fine if stopped by police)
- **Required documents:**
  - New proof of residence (lease, utility bill, property deed)

### Death Declaration
- **Deadline:** Within 24 hours
- **Where:** Town hall of death location
- **Required documents:**
  - Death certificate from doctor
  - Deceased's ID documents
  - Family record book

## üíº BUSINESS OBLIGATIONS

### Auto-Entrepreneur (Micro-Entreprise)

#### Registration
- **Deadline:** Before starting activity
- **Portal:** autoentrepreneur.urssaf.fr or guichet-entreprises.fr
- **Required documents:**
  - ID card
  - Proof of residence
  - Professional qualification certificates (for regulated activities)

#### Revenue Declaration
- **Frequency:** Monthly or quarterly (chosen at registration)
- **Deadlines:**
  - Monthly: Last day of each month
  - Quarterly: End of April, July, October, January
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - ‚Ç¨58 penalty per late declaration
  - 5% penalty on contributions due
  - 0.2% monthly interest

#### Annual Declaration
- **When:** April-May (income tax return)
- **Purpose:** Income tax calculation
- **Penalties:** Same as income tax penalties

### VAT (TVA - Taxe sur la Valeur Ajout√©e)
- **Threshold (2024):**

  - Above thresholds: Monthly or quarterly
  - CA12 form: Annually if exempt
- **Deadlines:**
  - Monthly: 24th of following month
  - Quarterly: 24th of month following quarter
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - 10% of tax due (minimum ‚Ç¨15)

### CFE (Cotisation Fonci√®re des Entreprises)
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Deadline:** December 15
- **First Year:** Exempt
- **Penalties if missed:**
  - 10% surcharge

- **Required documents:**

- **For Employers:** Monthly social security declarations
- **Deadline:** 5th or 15th of following month (depending on size)
- **Platform:** net-entreprises.fr
- **Penalties:** Significant fines for late/missing declarations
