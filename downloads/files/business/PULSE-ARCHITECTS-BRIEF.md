### E-commerce flow
1. Customer browses catalog (filterable by category, specs, price)
2. Adds plans to cart (Zustand store, persisted to localStorage)
3. Checkout: enters details, selects payment (Card or PayPal via Stripe)
4. Order confirmed, license key generated
5. Instant digital delivery with secure download links
6. Download tracking and email confirmation sent

### Dashboard capabilities (Admin)
- Analytics: total users, images uploaded, downloads today, total downloads
- Track downloads by license type
- Image status management (Approved, Pending, Rejected)
- Content upload and moderation
- Site settings configuration

## 13. FAQ REFERENCE

**Q: Can plans be customized?**
A: Yes. All plans can be customized for specific needs, site requirements, and design preferences. Licensed architects modify existing plans or create fully custom designs.

**Q: What's included in architectural plans?**
A: Floor plans, elevations, sections, construction details, structural drawings, electrical layouts, plumbing plans, material specifications, 3D visualizations, and building permit-ready documents.

**Q: How long does custom design take?**
A: Standard modifications: 2-3 weeks. Fully custom designs: 6-12 weeks depending on complexity. Detailed timelines provided during initial consultation.

**Q: Is construction support available?**
A: Yes. Contractor selection assistance, construction document review, site visits, and quality control inspections are offered.
